About Our Service

Revitalize your sofas with our expert cleaning services. We remove dirt, stains, and odors, ensuring a fresh and comfortable living space.

Cleaning Procedure

Step 1: Vacuuming the sofa to remove debris and dust.
Step 2: Pre-treating stains with safe cleaning solutions.
Step 3: Steam cleaning or dry cleaning based on fabric type.
Step 4: Drying and deodorizing for a fresh finish.
